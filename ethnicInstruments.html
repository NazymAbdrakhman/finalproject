Ethnic Instruments

Any folk musical instrument is a part of the history of an ethnos. They are able to reveal the specifics of morals and customs, to tell
a lot about the culture of their people. For example, Kazakhstan folk instruments discover the richness of the Kazakhstan soul, its bright creative morals. Confirmation
of this is the melodic character of Kazakhstan music, its polyphony.
